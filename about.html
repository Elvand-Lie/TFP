Our Story

Not Fortune
Telling.
Strategic
Intelligence.

THE FULL PICTURE was founded on a single conviction: that the ancient metaphysical systems of China — BaZi, Zi Wei Dou Shu, Qi Men Dun Jia, I Ching — are not relics of superstition, but sophisticated analytical frameworks developed over five millennia to understand the patterns of time, nature, and human potential.

We work with high-achieving individuals, business leaders, and families who recognise that true clarity comes not from guesswork or generalised advice, but from a structured understanding of their own energetic composition, their timing cycles, and the environment they operate within.

"The most powerful strategic advantage is knowing when to act, when to hold, and when to reposition — with precision."

Our practice bridges the philosophical depth of classical Chinese thought with the practical demands of modern advisory. We do not speak in the language of fate or superstition. We speak in the language of timing, leverage, alignment, and clarity — because that is what these systems, properly applied, actually deliver.

Every engagement is bespoke. Every recommendation is grounded in rigorous analysis. Every client we work with should feel, at the conclusion of our sessions, that they have seen their situation — and their potential — with a new and unflinching clarity.
