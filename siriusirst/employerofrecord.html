What is EOR

Your Company Directs.

An Employer of Record (EOR) allows your company to hire employees in Singapore without setting up a local entity. Sirius First becomes the legal employer while your company manages the employee's day-to-day work.

We hold the employment contract, run payroll, manage CPF contributions, and own every compliance obligation — leaving you free to focus on the work itself.
