Visa Pathways

Key Visa Categories

A clear overview of the pathways available to international professionals and their families. Our advisors will guide you to the most suitable route.

Employment Pass

Employment Pass (EP)

The primary pathway for foreign professionals, managers, and executives establishing their careers in Singapore. Suited to senior hires and founders entering through a Singapore-registered entity.

S Pass

S Pass

For skilled professionals employed by Singapore companies. Appropriate for specialist and technical roles within the mid-level salary band, subject to company quota.

Dependant Pass

Dependant Pass (DP)

For family members — spouses and children — relocating alongside visa holders. DP holders may be eligible to work in Singapore with a Letter of Consent.
